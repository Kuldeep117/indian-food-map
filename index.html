🏯 Rajasthan

🏛️ Capital: Jaipur

North India
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Dal Baati Churma - Traditional must try!
Laal Maas - Spicy mutton curry
Ghevar - Famous sweet
🍽️ Chokhi Dhani - Best traditional food

South India

Kerala Fish Curry - Must try!
Puttu & Kadala - Local favorite
